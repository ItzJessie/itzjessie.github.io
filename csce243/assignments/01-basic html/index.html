Welcome to Wakanda

Marvel Rivals brings the power and prestige of Wakanda into battle through two popular game modes. The first is Quickplay, a 6v6 objective-based mode where heroes clash in fast-paced combat inspired by Wakanda’s advanced technology and strategic warfare. The second is Competitive, also a 6v6 objective-based mode, but with a ranking system that challenges players to rise through the ranks and prove themselves worthy of Wakanda’s elite. These modes can be highly intense, as every player’s decision can shift the tide of battle and determine whether Wakanda stands victorious or falls.

Marvel Rivals is supported globally, allowing warriors from Wakanda and beyond to join the fight.The game supports a wide range of systems, including PCs, laptops, PS5, Xbox, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring access to Wakanda’s battles from almost anywhere. Marvel Rivals is optimized with low system requirements, giving more players the opportunity to experience Wakanda’s advanced combat and technology. In addition, the game offers five global server regions, providing smooth performance and a strong gaming experience worthy of Wakanda’s standard of excellence.
