Japanese Animation History Archive

2010s

Key Developments

  • Streaming platforms and simulcasts make new episodes available worldwide, nearly day-and-date with Japan
  • Digital pipelines and compositing become the default, accelerating production and enabling hybrid 2D/3D visuals
  • The isekai boom reshapes TV lineups, while seasonal cour formats dominate scheduling
  • Anime becomes a mainstream global entertainment category with large conventions and social media fandoms

Influential Series

2013 - Attack on Titan

A breakout dark fantasy series that helped define the decade's global anime conversation.

2011 - Puella Magi Madoka Magica

A genre-bending magical girl series known for its darker themes and stylish direction.

2019 - Demon Slayer

A blockbuster shounen hit praised for its cinematic action and stunning animation.
