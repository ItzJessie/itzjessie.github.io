Japanese Animation History Archive

2000s

Key Developments

  • Digital production pipelines become the standard, speeding up TV schedules and expanding visual styles
  • Long-running shounen hits like Naruto, One Piece, and Bleach dominate global fandoms
  • Light novel and manga adaptations fuel a wave of genre variety, from dark thrillers to school dramas
  • International releases grow through DVDs and early streaming, widening access beyond Japan

Influential Series

2001 - Spirited Away

An Academy Award-winning film that brought global attention to anime's storytelling power.

2004 - Fullmetal Alchemist

A richly plotted fantasy series that blended action, philosophy, and sibling drama.

2006 - Death Note

A tense cat-and-mouse thriller that became a breakout hit worldwide.
